Research

My research interests mainly lie in natural language processing and machine translation. Particularly, I am interested in:

  • Speech-to-text translation and speech-to-speech translation
  • Large language models (LLMs) for machine translation / speech translation
  • Multimodal (audio, visual, etc.) learning for machine translation
